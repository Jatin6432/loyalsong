Metal

Arjun has been a metal-head for a long time; firstly dabbling with Canadian rockers No Ones Alone, then Canadian melodic death-metallers Calculating Collapse.

His latest metallic endeavour is the thrashy power-metal project Pramata.

Fusing the power of early thrash-metal with classic, huge choruses, Pramata introduces the thematic elements of Indian mythology into the existing metal paradigm.
