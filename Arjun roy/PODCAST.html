HOMETOWN HUSTLE W / Arjun Roy

This is a conversation series with an accompanying podcast that charts my artistic journey through the Peace Country art & music scene circa year-2000 onwards. I believe in the importance of interconnectedness and collaboration, and thus am fascinated by art and music scenes and how they provide a space for budding artists to cut their teeth, stretch out and find themselves. I found exactly that upon moving to Grande Prairie in Autumn of 2000.

Meet some of my dear friends and collaborators and watch/listen as we reminisce about coming up as artist & musicians, developing our craft, working on our art and everything that went along with that process, warts and all. Hindsight is 20/20 and there is SO much wisdom to be gleaned from our experiences.

EPISODE 08 - Budi and I go way back, first meeting in the mid-2000s and playing out lots during the heyday of the GP and area music scene.
