ARJUN ROY

Currently based in Canada, Arjun’s journey has taken him across the globe for reasons musical and otherwise. Exposed to a wide variety of music from an early age, Arjun began guitar lessons at the age of 12 and hasn’t looked back. Initially on track to pursue a career in the Sciences, Arjun made a decision to switch paths early on, and dive wholeheartedly into music…somehow.

This leap of faith led to a burgeoning career spanning song-writing, live performance, production, recording, engineering, mixing, curriculum development and consultancy covering Canada, the USA and Europe, including degrees from Berklee College Of Music in Boston (BM) and Berklee Valencia (MM).
